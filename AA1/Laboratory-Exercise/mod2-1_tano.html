Charlotte

Charlotte follows four students in a fictional story set at Hoshinoumi Academy, a school established to protect children who develop supernatural abilities as they reach puberty. The background of Yuu is revealed as the plot progresses, and the past is tampered with. A decision is made to protect those with supernatural abilities, but everything has consequences.
