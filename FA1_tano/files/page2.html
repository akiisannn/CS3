Taylor Swift- Cruel Summer

  • On August 23, 2019, Taylor Swift released Cruel Summer, the second track from her full album "Lover"

  • I discovered this song by shuffling all of Taylor's songs and i loved it at first listen. The song has this nostalgic
    and ecstatic feeling.

  • The song talks about romance during summer and the difficulties the singer experienced during the romance.
    The hook is so catchy and satisfying to listen to that I can't get enough of it!
    The lyric "I love you. Ain't that the worst thing you ever heard?" hits so hard too. Since this
    song talks about the pain in summer romance, it makes sense that the lyrics has this kind of
    regret or realization that love is complicated.
