TXT- Our Summer(Acoustic Mix)

  • On February 12, 2019, TXT released the acoustic mix of one of their debut songs, Our Summer.

  • I discovered this song when listening to their debut single for the first time. The song is very bright
    and innocent sounding, that it brightens my day.

  • The song talks about a person that makes you feel like it's summer everytime with their love.
    The song has a carefree feeling and the lyrics also reflect that, such as the lyrics
    "Blooming gold season, like our summer/No matter where you are, no matter what season/If we're together, feel like summer"
