NIKI- Backburner

  • On August 12, 2022, NIKI released Backburner, the third track from her full album "Nicole"

  • I discovered this song while streaming the entire album because I was already a fan of NIKI.
    I love the chorus and word plays from Reformation, and it makes more sense to me now that
    we just learned about it in social science.

  • The song talks about being used by your partner only when they need something from you.
    Because backburner means "low priority" or "not important," when NIKI sings "But guess
    I won't ever mind crisping up on your backburner," she means that she will stay even though
    she is considered low priority. The song is not romantically relatable to me, but it is relatable
    to my social life. I like the song because of the lyricism and the use of wordplay, particularly
    the line "Hey! Are you still there?" because the song becomes choppy as if she is being disconnected.
